About Us


We are ReplicationProject, a service that enables the secure and reliable sharing of documents over a distributed network. Our goal is to simplify the search and download of documents stored on multiple replicas, ensuring fast and reliable access.


Our Mission


We are committed to offering a decentralized document sharing experience, ensuring an efficient and secure service. By connecting to distributed nodes, we enable users to access the documents they want in a reliable way.


How does it work?


After registering on a central server, users can search for documents by name. We contact the active nodes to find and download the requested document from one of the selected nodes, optimizing the process to ensure a fast download and canceling requests to other nodes.
